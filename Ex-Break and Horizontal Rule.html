Short Poems:

Weeping

by Federico GorcAo Lorco

Weeping,
I go down the street
Grotesque,without solution
with the sadness of Cyrano
And Quixote.


Redeeming
Infinite impossibilities
With the rhytm of the clock.

String Bass

by Adrian Green

Some like to dominate,
others caress
a voluptuous rhytm
on pliant strings. This pulse drives life
through wanton counterpoint,
the heart and harmony
of things.
